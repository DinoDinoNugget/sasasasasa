Little River Elementary School

This is my School?

Little River Elementary School is located in South Riding, Va. The school provides education to students in kindergarten through fifth grade. Its core curriculum includes English, math, science, social studies and language arts. Little River Elementary School offers special programs in various subjects, such as art, guidance, music, physical education, reading and technology. The school s guidance department organizes counseling sessions for students and parents. It has a library that includes textbooks, storybooks, picture dictionaries, and fiction and nonfiction books. Little River Elementary School s parent-teacher association sponsors several events, including fundraising activities, scholastic book fairs, spaghetti dinners, staff appreciation weeks and art displays.
